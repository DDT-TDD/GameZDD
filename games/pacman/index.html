🎮 PAC-MAN 🎮

Classic arcade maze chase game

📝 How to Play

Guide Pac-Man through the maze, eat all the dots, and avoid the ghosts!

↑ ↓ ← → - Move Pac-Man
SPACE - Pause/Resume
ENTER - Start Game
Power Pellet - Eat ghosts!

🔵 Eat the large power pellets to turn the ghosts blue and make them vulnerable!
