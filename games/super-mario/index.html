🎮 SUPER MARIO - Realistic Platformer 🎮

Game Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD: Move left and right

Space / Up Arrow: Jump (hold for higher jump)

Jump on enemies to defeat them

Collect coins for bonus points

Game Speed

1.0x

Game Features

  • ✅ Realistic gravity and jump physics (like Super Mario Bros)
  • ✅ Hold-to-jump for variable jump height
  • ✅ Jump buffering and coyote time (forgiving controls)
  • ✅ Wall sliding mechanics
  • ✅ Wall jumping for advanced movement
  • ✅ Enemy goombas to jump on
  • ✅ Coin collection system
  • ✅ Smooth animations and particle effects
  • ✅ Multiple progressively challenging levels
  • ✅ Score tracking and lives system

Physics Details

  • ⚙️ Gravity: 600 pixels/second²
  • ⚙️ Terminal velocity: 400 pixels/second
  • ⚙️ Jump power: 320 pixels/second (hold for higher)
  • ⚙️ Max jump height: ~120 pixels
  • ⚙️ Coyote time: 6 frames (forgiveness after leaving ground)
  • ⚙️ Jump buffer: 100ms window for responsive controls
  • ⚙️ Wall friction: 90% velocity retention (slow slides)
