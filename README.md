## 🎮 List of Playable Games

### 🧩 Maze & Logic Puzzles

#### 1. **Pac-Man** 👻
Navigate mazes, eat dots, avoid ghosts, and grab power-ups!
- **Features:** Classic ghost AI, power pellets, multiple layouts, high-score tracking.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 2. **Maze Munch (Pac-Man Maze)** 🟡
Procedurally generated mazes with randomized layouts, power pellets, and fruits!
- **Features:** 4 ghost colors, progressive difficulty levels, procedural map builder.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 3. **Hero Quest (Lolo)** 🗡️
Explore grid-based levels, solve box pushing mechanics, and dodge enemies to open chests!
- **Features:** Room navigation, puzzle designs, custom layouts, level progression.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 4. **Tunnel Quest (Mole Mania)** ⛏️
Dig your way through soil, collect hidden diamonds and gems, and dodge falling rocks!
- **Features:** Destructible soil, physical falling objects, multi-stage tunnel maps.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 5. **Box Pusher (Sokoban)** 📦
Push heavy crates onto designated target locations in a logical, smart puzzle game!
- **Features:** Turn tracking, reset-level trigger, progressive retro map layouts.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 6. **Sokoban Master** 🧩
100 classic levels of the ultimate brain workout! Track your moves and target positions.
- **Features:** Level selector, movement and push metrics counter, persistent target tracking.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

### 🏃 Platform Action Puzzles

#### 7. **Super Jumper (Super Mario Style)** 🍄
Full-physics platformer. Leap between bricks, collect shiny gold coins, and hop on enemies!
- **Features:** Acceleration, momentum, custom level configurations, and responsive jumping.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD, Space (Jump).

#### 8. **Barrel Climber (Donkey Kong Style)** 🪜
Climb sturdy steel ladders, dodge rolling barrels, and aim for the high platform to win!
- **Features:** Gravity-based obstacles, moving hazards, scaling ladders.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD, Space (Jump).

#### 9. **Chef Rush (BurgerTime Style)** 🍔
Work as a master chef, walking across burger ingredients to assemble burgers!
- **Features:** Ladder scaling, enemies, ingredient cascade physics, combo score multiplier.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD.

#### 10. **Sailor Quest (Popeye Style)** ⚓
Jump across floating platforms and gather scattered olives before the timer runs out!
- **Features:** Animated platform levels, dynamic collections, and lives tracker.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD, Space (Jump).

### 🫧 Bubble & Block Puzzles

#### 11. **Block Fall (Tetris)** 🎮
Classic block puzzle! Arrange blocks to form complete lines. Includes custom helpful settings.
- **Features:** Hold piece slot, bag-randomizer queue, ghost placement visual helper, Kids Mode.
- **Controls:** Arrow Keys/WASD (Move/Rotate), C (Hold), Space (Hard Drop).

#### 12. **Memory Cards (Card Matching)** 🃏
Flip cards, match identical characters or shapes, and aim for 100% accuracy!
- **Features:** Easy/Medium/Hard difficulty, click speed tracking, animated flip effects.
- **Controls:** Mouse Click.

#### 13. **Block Blast** 🟦
Drop blocks onto grid cells and clear connected color groupings! Cascade matching for bonus points.
- **Features:** Next piece previews, modern grid mechanics, and dynamic color combos.
- **Controls:** Mouse Click / Drag.

#### 14. **Brick Breaker (Arkanoid Style)** 🏓
Bounce balls off your paddle to smash beautiful bricks. Catch falling power-ups!
- **Features:** Paddle expansion, multi-ball, slow-ball, extra-life, power-ball, 5 beautiful themes.
- **Controls:** Mouse Move / Keyboard Arrow Keys.

#### 15. **Bubble Pop (Puzzle Bubble Style)** 🫧
Aim your launcher bubble and shoot to match three or more of the same colored bubbles!
- **Features:** Physic trajectory guide, colorful matching logic, bubble popping animations.
- **Controls:** Mouse Move & Click.

#### 16. **Marble Chain (Zuma Style)** ⚽
Shoot colored marbles into incoming chains before they reach the critical track exit!
- **Features:** Curved ball tracks, multiplier combos, marble matching logic.
- **Controls:** Mouse/Arrows (Aim), Space (Shoot).

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. Run the [Launch GameZDD.bat](Launch%20GameZDD.bat) file inside the project directory, or double-click [launcher.html](launcher.html) to play inside your favorite browser!
2. Click on the ⚙️ Settings icon to customize Player Name, sound options, or music volume.
3. Select any game and click "PLAY" or "CONTINUE" to resume your progress.

## ✨ Educational Features

GameZDD helps develop critical logic skills:
- **Spatial Reasoning** - Estimating angles, planning block placements, rotating shapes.
- **Problem Solving** - Route planning, figuring out box pushing rules, analyzing chain reactions.
- **Hand-Eye Coordination** - Platforming jumps, paddle alignments, timing marble shots.
- **Offline & Kid-Safe** - 100% ad-free, offline execution, zero data collection.

---

## 📁 Project Structure

All shared systems reside in the standard engine folder:
- [engine/audioManager.js](engine/audioManager.js) - Music & sound effects controller.
- [engine/inputHandler.js](engine/inputHandler.js) - Cross-browser input mapping wrapper.
- [engine/saveManager.js](engine/saveManager.js) - LocalStorage-backed save slot tracker.
- [launcher.js](launcher.js) - UI mapping for all 16 games.
